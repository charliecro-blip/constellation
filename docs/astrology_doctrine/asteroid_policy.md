# Asteroid Policy

Asteroids and advanced points are supporting material by default. They can add precision, but they should not override primary synastry, house, angle, Saturn, or composite evidence.

## Default / MVP points

- Juno.
- Chiron.
- Ceres.
- Vesta, when relationship relevance is clear.

## Advanced / deferred points

- Eros.
- Psyche.
- Lilith.
- Vertex.
- Asteroid-to-asteroid contacts.

## Surfacing rules

Asteroid material needs tight orbs, central personal targets, and relationship-relevant houses to appear in default reports. Stronger targets include Sun, Moon, Venus, Mars, Ascendant, and Descendant. Relevant houses include the 1st, 5th, 7th, 8th, and 12th.

Advanced points are suppressed by default because they can be vivid but easy to overstate. They belong in an explicit advanced mode or in tightly supported cases, not in the main report opening.

Avoid sensational language, especially around Lilith or Pluto. Do not turn asteroid contacts into claims about fate, sexual destiny, obsession, or danger.
