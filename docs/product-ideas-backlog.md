## "Tell me about..." focused topic threads

**Idea:** Instead of (or in addition to) generating a full Relationship Map immediately, let a user search or navigate thematic threads — e.g., "communication," "sex/intimacy," "emotional needs," "conflict patterns," "money/resources" — and see only the portions of the map relevant to that thread.

**Shape:** A set of named lenses or topic cards that filter and reorder the report's sections, dynamic details, and pattern evidence around a single relational question. Each lens might weave together synastry, overlay, composite, and temperament material that bears on that topic.

**Why interesting:** Many users don't want to absorb a full map on first visit. A topic-first entry point could lower the cognitive load and let people explore their own questions rather than reading a prescribed narrative order.

**Technical consideration:** The underlying data already exists — patterns, dynamic details, diagnostics, and synthesis packets all carry topic signals (categories, keys, evidence text). A lens layer would filter and re-sequence that material without changing the underlying engine.

**Not for current build:** No UI infrastructure yet. Consider after the report engine is trusted and a product shell (auth, saved maps, dashboard) is in place.
